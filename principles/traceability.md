# Traceability

**Movement:** I — Ground · [Index](../README.md)

> **Invariant:** Every inference earns its chain: a conclusion is
> trustworthy only when its path back to ground or principle is explicit
> and auditable.

## The universal

Reasoning has a static face and a dynamic face. Position — what is
actually true, what is actually needed — is [Grounding](grounding.md)'s
charge. Momentum — reasoning forward from that position — is this
principle's: every conclusion traces back through intermediate
inferences to verified ground or principle, and the chain is explicit
enough that another reader can audit it. A correct starting point does
not protect the reasoning that leaves it; fluent, locally-valid
inference drifts from ground while feeling rigorous the whole way. A
chain that cannot be shown cannot be trusted, however correct its
conclusion happens to be.

The chain breaks in three ways: an inference follows from the previous
inference without tracing to ground (*chain drift*); an inference
follows from analogy rather than principle (*pattern projection*); a
principle is cited but does not govern — remove the citation and the
reasoning does not change (*principle as decoration*). And a chain valid
at every link can still terminate prematurely: a conclusion evaluated
only against the consequence that prompted the inquiry feels complete
because the chain was honest, but validity is not completeness. The
chain is finished when the conclusion has been checked against every
behavior it touches.

**Recognition:** A conclusion "feels right" or "follows naturally," but
the chain that produced it cannot be articulated. Or the chain is stated
and a link invokes analogy, precedent, or familiarity rather than ground
or principle. Or the chain closed after the consequence that prompted
the inquiry, without asking what else the conclusion touches.

**Corrective:** Rebuild the chain explicitly: for each inference, name
the ground or principle it follows from. If a link is missing or
analogical, re-derive from the last verified link. Before closing the
chain, enumerate the consequence surface and trace the paths it adds.
