# Completed Noticing

**Named composition** · [Index](../README.md)

> **Invariant:** A completion claim names what was attended, what was
> verified, how it responded, where it released, and what residue
> remains — or states explicitly where and why the cycle stopped.

Long form: *honest noticing, completed by action.* A five-move work
cycle composed of six existing universals. Each move's content lives in
its constituents — consult them by link; this document owns only what
no constituent holds: the completeness demand across all five moves,
and the non-possessive element of Release.

## The cycle

1. **Attend** — perceive the actual substrate, not the assumed one.
   → [Grounding](../principles/grounding.md).
2. **Verify** — check the perception against reality before acting on
   it. → Grounding's diagnostic face ∘
   [Verifiable Completion](../principles/verifiable-completion.md)
   (*verify before act*).
3. **Respond** — act proportionately to what was seen.
   → [Obligation to Dissent](../principles/obligation-to-dissent.md)
   (*fix, file, or halt*).
4. **Release** — make the action durable, successor-readable, and
   non-possessive. → Verifiable Completion ∘
   [Transmission](../principles/transmission.md).
5. **Repeat** — expose the residue to the next cycle.
   → [Recursive Improvement](../principles/recursive-improvement.md).

The ordering is owned by [Sequence](../principles/sequence.md): each
move produces the ground the next requires.

## What the composition itself owns

**The completeness invariant** (above). No constituent demands that a
completion claim traverse all five moves; the composition does. And no
move may silently substitute for another: planning is not response; a
local commit is not release; a comment is not verification; a closed
issue is not proof of completion.

**The non-possessive element of Release.** Durable work survives its
maker's absence — and is let go of: outcome, credit, identity. The
constituents' landing content covers durability and transmissibility;
non-possession is the composition's own addition.

## Failure diagnostic

Each missing move corrupts the cycle in a named way:

- *Attend without Verify:* delusion — projection mistaken for
  perception.
- *Verify without Respond:* paralysis, cynicism — accurate seeing that
  changes nothing.
- *Respond without Release:* local-only work, possession, burnout — the
  fix exists only where its maker stands.
- *Release without Repeat:* brittle closure — learning lost at the
  moment it was earned.
- *Repeat without renewed attention:* ritualized drift — the cycle's
  form without its perception.
